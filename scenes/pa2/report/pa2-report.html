Part 3: Improved ray traversal ====================== * Now I add an ordered traversal of child nodes. First, I check if the ray intersect bounding boxes of children. I also get the near intersections. Then, I sort the nodes in an ascending order based on the near intersection. After that, I do ordered traversal. As bounding boxes of children nodes don't overlap each other, if I can find an intersection face in a previous child, I won't be able to find a nearer intersection face in a following child.
